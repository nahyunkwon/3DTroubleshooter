3DPFIX Introduction
Does your 3D print have a problem, and you do not know what to do to fix it? Get immediate help from 3DPFIX! Using 3DPFIX, you do not need to struggle to recognize what the problem is by searching for online tutorials or waiting for the comments in online communities.
3DPFIX provides an automatic diagnosis of common printing failures powered by AI from the photo of your failed print. Simply take a photo of your print, preferably centering the print with a clear background, and upload the photo(s) to get the diagnosis. Please allow some time, but less than a minute, to get the diagnosis.
3DPFIX also supports you to decide what to do to resolve the problem. Go over the general solutions for the diagnosed problem that will highly likely resolve most of the cases. Did you spot any notable issues (e.g., clicking sound, wobbling printing bed, etc.) when you printed the 3D model? Those issues will also help 3DPFIX find a customized solution for your specific case. So, please check if there is an issue that matches what you spotted. 3DPFIX will show the matching solutions that you can try first. Click the 'tutorial' button in each solution card, it will lead you to an adequate web tutorial for that solution if you need more details.
You are very new to 3D printing, and even do not know the basic technical terms in 3D printing? No worries, 3DPFIX will support you to learn the technical terms as well. Just like Wikipedia, put your mouse pointer on the term, and it will show a detailed explanation.
Happy printing with 3DPFIX!
